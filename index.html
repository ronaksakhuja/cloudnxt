    Why Cloudnxt

    There is no doubt that every business runs on information and store a large amount of data from their business operations. So, it is essential that businesses find ways to secure that massive data and at the same time have easy access to them. In other words, without comprehensive cloud management, businesses are susceptible to security threats, data loss and costly downtime. This is the area where Cloudnxt Solutions, LTD. is specialized and create value for you. We are known as the one that has extensive cloud knowledge that provides support to the business’ operations related with all IT related matters.

    A recent survey by Frost & Sullivan reported that 80% of businesses are planning to increase their use of cloud managed services. The two main factors driving this increase are - 1. Cloud computing is very complex and 2. Businesses realize the importance of cloud management.

    With cloud, businesses know they have a tremendous technology delivery model at their fingertips, but they don’t always know how to manage it. They might not have the expertise on staff. The self-service cloud might be more complex than they expected. Additionally, the stakes for getting it right are high. As a result, they’re turning to specialists, like us, who can provide the management overlay to make sure that workloads are secure, efficient and cost controlled. Research in this matter have clearly shown that the following 3 trends will become evident in year 2019:

    1. Growth in cloud computing: A recent report by International Data Corporation indicates that “Worldwide spending on public cloud services and infrastructure is forecast to reach $266 billion in 2021. The cloud computing market is expected to achieve a five-year compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 21.0% and public cloud services spending reached $128 billion in 2017, an increase of 25.4% over 2016.” The meteoric rise of cloud computing is altering purchasing decisions and changing how solutions are delivered through the channel. This shift is presenting challenges and providing opportunities to managed service providers.

    2. Migration from private to public cloud infrastructure: While private cloud environments deliver a type of cloud computing that is similar to public clouds, they also require sophisticated virtualization, technical automation, resource monitoring, and many other expensive resources. Compared to a private cloud, the most obvious benefit to using a public cloud is the savings. With a public cloud, organizations do not have to purchase, install, operate, or maintain servers or other equipment as they would with private clouds. As organizations seek to reduce their annual IT spend, public clouds offer a viable alternative that will grow significantly in 2019 and beyond.

    3. Increasing demand for secure IT infrastructure: 2017 includes string of high visibility malware attacks on a global scale that are of concern to companies that consider their IT environments critical to success. As a result, businesses will look to managed service providers in 2019 to ensure that secure service infrastructure is in place before making their provider selection. Security is not only dependent on the security solutions in place but also on the overall IT environment and how it is set up to both defend and recover against such attacks. As such, managed service provider business models that take this into consideration will lead with a strong point of differentiation in 2019 and beyond.
